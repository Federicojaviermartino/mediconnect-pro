Privacy Policy

MediConnect Pro - Protecting Your Health Information

Last Updated: October 16, 2025

Privacy Commitment

MediConnect Pro is committed to protecting your privacy and health data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights over your data.

By using MediConnect Pro, you accept the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Protected Health Information (PHI)

We collect personal health information as defined by HIPAA and other applicable regulations:

  • Personal Identification: Name, date of birth, address, phone, email
  • Medical Information: Medical history, diagnoses, treatments, allergies, medications
  • Vital Signs: Blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, weight, height
  • Consultation Information: Medical notes, transcriptions, images, test results
  • Insurance Information: Insurance provider, policy number, eligibility
  • Prescription Information: Medications, dosages, instructions

1.2 Account and Authentication Information

  • Login credentials (email, hashed password)
  • User role (patient, doctor, administrator)
  • Account preferences
  • Session history

1.3 AI-Generated Information

When using our AI tools, we collect:

  • Symptoms described for triage
  • Consultation transcriptions
  • Generated medical notes
  • Generated medical reports
  • AI interaction inputs and outputs

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Provision of Medical Services

  • Facilitate medical consultations between patients and professionals
  • Maintain your electronic medical record
  • Process medical prescriptions
  • Monitor vital signs
  • Generate medical reports
  • Coordinate medical care

2.2 AI Assistance

We use your health data with AI services for:

  • Symptom Triage: Assess urgency and recommend specialist
  • Transcription: Convert consultation audio to text
  • Note Generation: Create structured medical notes
  • Medical Reports: Generate medical history summaries

Important About Data and AI

When possible, we anonymize or pseudonymize data before sending it to third-party AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic). However, some medical contexts require identifiable data to be clinically useful.

AI providers are subject to their own privacy policies:

  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy
  • Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy

3. Who We Share Your Information With

Category Purpose Shared Data
Medical Professionals Provision of medical care Complete medical history, vital signs, prescriptions
AI Providers AI assistance Symptoms, transcriptions, clinical data (anonymized when possible)
Pharmacies E-prescriptions Prescription information, necessary patient data
Insurance Companies Eligibility verification, claims Information necessary for coverage and billing
Legal Authorities Legal compliance Information required by law or court order

4. Data Security

4.1 Technical Measures

  • Encryption in transit: HTTPS/TLS for all communications
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data encrypted in database
  • Secure authentication: Passwords hashed with bcrypt
  • Secure sessions: httpOnly, sameSite, secure cookies
  • Monitoring: Detection of suspicious activities

4.2 Security Limitations

Important Notice

While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security against all cyber risks.

In case of a data breach affecting your PHI, we will notify you as required by law (HIPAA requires notification within 60 days).

5. Your Rights Over Your Data

5.1 Rights Under HIPAA (US)

  • Right of Access: Obtain a copy of your medical records
  • Right of Correction: Request amendments to incorrect information
  • Right to Accounting: Know who has accessed your PHI
  • Right of Restriction: Request to limit certain uses/disclosures
  • Right to Complain: File complaints without retaliation

5.2 Rights Under GDPR (Europe)

  • Right of Access: Confirm what data we have about you
  • Right of Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of data (with exceptions)
  • Right of Portability: Receive your data in structured format
  • Right of Objection: Object to certain processing

5.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@mediconnect.demo

We will respond to your request within 30 days (HIPAA) or 1 month (GDPR), with possible extensions as permitted by law.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do:

  • We will post the updated version on our platform
  • We will update the "Last Updated" date
  • For material changes, we will notify you by email or prominent notice
  • Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance

  • Email: privacy@mediconnect.demo

By using MediConnect Pro, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
